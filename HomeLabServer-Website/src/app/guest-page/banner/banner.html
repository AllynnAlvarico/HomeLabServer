Welcome to my Homelab Server Project!

This is a personal project and my own way to learn hands-on server management and full-stack development. If you’re into tech, check out what I’m experimenting with, Ubuntu Server to automation with bash and n8n. I’m always learning new things and open to ideas or collabs!
I kept track of most of what I did setting this up, so if you want to follow along or build your own homelab, just click the button and you’ll find all the steps I used.
